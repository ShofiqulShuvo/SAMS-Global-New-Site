The SAMS Global ecosystem is designed to take a 360 degree view of international student recruitment. We are not just interested in student mobility but want to offer innovative solutions across all areas of student recruitment, to all stakeholders. Our combined experience in the sector puts us in a great place to understand the pinch points that hinder growth and development for our partners; and our technical teams have developed deep insights, vastly aiding our R&D goals.

A trusted partner in higher education

With over 50 direct university partners and another 50+ through our strategic partnerships, we help process thousands of applications each year through our network of over 600, carefully selected agent partners. This gives the perfect opportunity for us to gain deep insights into the student journey.

Our CRM client base is growing rapidly, with over 1000 users, based in 49 countries around the world, taking advantage of our fully mapped, integration rich, seamless lead and application management platform. Our sub-agent, event management and soon to come on stream, accounts management features are industry leading.

We have managed to take our insights to new heights with our unique commission processing platform. A pinch point that is understood by almost all universities, we are gaining traction at record speed with universities reaching out to us more and more. We are integrating this into our brand new agent management suite of solutions, being designed with 7 university partners.
