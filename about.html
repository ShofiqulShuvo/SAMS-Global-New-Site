A quality first approach

SAMS Global is an EdTech Company specialising in all aspects of International student mobility that brings together student recruitment agents, universities and students in one place; offering 4 bespoke products spanning the entire student journey.

In 2019 SAMS Global Solutions was formed and we launched our Apply and CRM products in 2020. The SAMS Pay solution was subsequently launched in 2023. Our senior team has been in the international student recruitment space since 2001, with our CEO forming his first recruitment agency in 2005.

The aim with SAMS Global, was not to just make something that works, but to produce solutions, that are innovative and insightful. In terms of recruitment, agents are able to combine the power of our CRM with a swift and service-oriented platform to process applications whilst concentrating on nurturing the student’s journey or use both products totally independently. Meanwhile SAMS Pay is attracting interest from a variety of institutions, across several countries and we have a working group of 7 universities collaborating with us on our SAMS360 suite of solutions.
